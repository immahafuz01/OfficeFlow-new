📋 Project Brief: OfficeFlow
App Name: OfficeFlow
Tagline: “Simplified Office Accounting – Smart, Secure & Always Up-to-Date”
Project Overview:
OfficeFlow is a complete accounting and financial management solution designed for small and medium-sized offices. It will help business owners, managers, and accountants easily track income, expenses, payroll, invoices, and generate professional reports — all in one platform. The system will be available on both Mobile App and Web Dashboard.
Target Users:
Small to medium offices (5–50 employees), shops, trading businesses, coaching centers, startups, and freelance teams in Bangladesh and similar markets.

🎯 Core Features

Dashboard
Real-time overview of today’s income & expenses
Cash flow chart, monthly summary, total balance
Quick statistics and alerts

Transaction Management
Add Income & Expense entries
Cash & Bank account tracking
Categories & Sub-categories
Party/Customer ledger

Invoicing & Billing
Create professional invoices
Generate PDF invoices
Invoice status (Paid, Unpaid, Overdue)
Payment recording

Reporting
Profit & Loss Statement
Balance Sheet
Expense breakdown reports (with charts)
Daily, Monthly, Yearly reports
Export to PDF & Excel

User & Role Management
Multi-user support
Roles: Admin, Accountant, Viewer
Permission-based access

Additional Features (Phase 2)
Employee payroll & salary management
Basic inventory linking
Bank reconciliation
Dark mode
Bengali + English language support
